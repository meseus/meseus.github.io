Why collaborate with our network?

Following Facebook-Cambridge Analytica awareness has grown regarding the silos of personal information collected by big tech companies. Alternatives like selling or syndicating your personal preferences have proven unfitted by privacy and security standards.
Our approach involves opening a channel for every user where there publishers can leverage an encrypted version of the personal data preferences, such as favorite movies or personal skills. Our disruptive decentralized network of publishers and consumers, preserves privacy and leveraging machine learning techniques for automatic personalized recommendations. Applications range from job search and movies to boutique wines and luxury cars.

Design

Bleeding edge decentralized machine learning algorithms and a distributed filesystem, assures scalable and fast retrieval of recommendations, without intermediaries.

Feedback

User and publishers are involved in a Token Market, that provides feedback about preferences and delivered value.

Execution

Use of blockchain for immutable Token Registries and a hash-based recommender system established high-standards for privacy of your data as consumer and proof of relevant results for publishers.
